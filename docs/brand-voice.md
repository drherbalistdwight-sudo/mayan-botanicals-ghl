# Brand Voice

## Brand
Mayan Botanicals

## Founder
Docta Herbalist Dwight

## Voice Attributes
- Warm
- Natural
- Supportive
- Grounded
- Trustworthy
- Calm
- Educational

## Tone Guidelines
- Speak with clarity and confidence
- Focus on guidance, not hype
- Use wellness language, not medical promises
- Sound human and reassuring
- Emphasize education, support, and personal wellness

## Preferred Phrases
- natural wellness support
- personalized guidance
- structured herbal wellness
- many clients report meaningful improvements
- rooted in tradition
- healthier daily habits
