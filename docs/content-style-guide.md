# Content Style Guide

## Writing Style
- Keep language clear and supportive
- Use short paragraphs
- Focus on wellness, not medical outcomes
- Write in a warm, trustworthy voice
- Emphasize guidance, education, and natural support

## Preferred Themes
- natural wellness
- balanced living
- herbal support
- personalized guidance
- healthier daily habits
- rooted in tradition

## Avoid
- medical promises
- aggressive hype
- disease reversal language
- guaranteed outcomes
- fear-based claims

## CTA Style
Use direct, calm CTA language:
- Take Free Wellness Assessment
- Book Free Consultation
- Explore Wellness Resources
